Tasks

You can train algorithmic tasks in BOS (Bulgarian olympic system). In order to do so, you have to register in the system by clicking here and register for the competition "Алгоритми". Keep in mind that the passwords are saved in plain text without hashing or encoding. So it is better to enter a password different from your other passwords! Also the username and password should be between 3 and 20 characters.

After you have registered, you can enter the system with your credentials and you can access all tasks in the competition "Алгоритми". The tasks are grouped by lessons as it is described at the end of every lesson page in this site.

If you only want to have a look at the competition system, there is a test contest with one task and a test user. Again you have to enter the system by clicking here. The name of the test user is test and the password is test123
